# DEC-0065 — Wealth visibility for negative-balance asset accounts

**Status:** Accepted  
**Date:** 2026-06-05  
**Context:** BUG-0010 AB — primary Giro account (114) excluded from wealth by `COALESCE(balance,0) >= 0` filter  
**Research:** [R-0062](../docs/engineering/research.md#r-0062--firefly-account-balance-mirror-vs-forecastwealth-inputs)

## Decision

Include **all active asset accounts** with `include_net_worth=true` in wealth breakdown regardless of sign. Mark accounts with `balance < 0` as `is_overdrawn: true` in API response. **Signed sum** contributes to `firefly.subtotal_eur` and `total_eur` (negative reduces net worth).

## Rationale

- Firefly asset accounts may legitimately show negative `current_balance` (overdraft, ccAsset debt-as-asset model).
- Silently omitting the primary checking account produces `total_eur: 0` and unusable wealth UX while forecast still lists the account.
- Extends DEC-0025 mixed-currency aggregation; does **not** integrate liability accounts.

## Alternatives rejected

| Alternative | Why rejected |
|-------------|--------------|
| Clamp negative balances to 0 at ingest | Distorts Firefly source of truth; breaks forecast starting balance |
| Separate liability integration | Epic scope; out of BUG-0010 |
| Show negative accounts in separate "overdrawn" section only, exclude from total | Operator expects signed net worth including checking overdraft |

## Risks

- Mixed-currency signed sum remains approximate (DEC-0025 banner unchanged).
- Large negative balance dominates headline total — mitigated by per-row `is_overdrawn` flag and AB2 empty-state guidance.

## Implementation anchor

- `backend/src/wealth/repository.rs` — remove `>= 0` filter
- `backend/src/wealth/types.rs` — add `is_overdrawn` on `AccountWealthRow`
- `frontend/src/pages/WealthPage.tsx` — visual cue for overdrawn rows
