# Decision DEC-0064

**Status:** Accepted  
**Date:** 2026-06-05  
**Bug:** BUG-0005  
**Research:** R-0059  
**Extends:** DEC-0038 (wealth analytics PnL boundary), DEC-0039 (FX pricing)

## Context

Bitunix futures ingestion adds both **wallet margin balances** (M1) and **open position rows** (N2). `WealthService` crypto subtotal sums all holdings with non-null `market_value_eur`. Wallet equity on Bitunix typically embeds unrealized PNL; pricing positions into the same subtotal would double-count exposure (discovery open question #2).

## Options

### 1. Wallet priced; positions unrealized-only (recommended)

| Source | `product_type` | `market_value_usd` | Wealth subtotal |
|--------|----------------|------------------|-----------------|
| Futures wallet (M1) | `futures` | Set for stablecoins (USDT ≈ USD) | **Included** |
| Open positions (N2) | `linear` | **`None`** | **Excluded** |
| Spot (existing) | `spot` | Per FX layer | Included when priced |

Positions remain visible in holdings list; unrealized PNL flows to portfolio engine per DEC-0038. Matches Binance connector pattern (positions without market_value in wealth sum).

**Alternative:** Positions-only, skip wallet — rejected (fails acceptance **M** margin balances).

**Alternative:** Price both wallet and notional position — rejected (double-count risk).

**Alternative:** Wallet None, positions only — rejected (margin collateral invisible in wealth).

### 2. MVP margin coin

USDT-only wallet query (`marginCoin=USDT`); multi-coin iteration deferred.

## Recommendation

Accept options **1** and **2**. Execute in Q0012 tasks **M1** and **N2**.

## Impact

- M1 sets `product_type: "futures"` with priced stablecoin wallet rows
- N2 sets `product_type: "linear"` with `market_value_usd: None`
- No `WealthService` aggregation change required (O1 verify only)

## Risks

- Holdings count may exceed priced subtotal rows — acceptable; O1 validates operator-visible combined exposure
- Alt-coin margin wallets omitted until multi-coin scope — document deferral
