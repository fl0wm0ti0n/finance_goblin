# Decision DEC-0017

**Status:** Accepted  
**Date:** 2026-05-31  
**Story:** US-0003  
**Research:** R-0011

## Context

Price-change detection on confirmed subscriptions must balance early warning against cent-level rounding noise. Discovery asked: any delta vs minimum €/percent threshold?

## Options

1. **Dual threshold: ≥€1.00 AND ≥5%** — Yodlee-style materiality (R-0011)
2. **Any amount delta** — rejected (rounding noise)
3. **Percent-only** — rejected (€0.50 on €5 sub = 10% but immaterial)
4. **US-0005 Alert Engine for MVP** — rejected (subscription-scoped alerts in backlog)

## Recommendation

Fire `price_increase` / `price_decrease` events and `subscription_alerts` when **both**:

- `|delta| ≥ subscription_price_change_min_eur` (default **€1.00**)
- `|delta| / |previous| × 100 ≥ subscription_price_change_min_pct` (default **5%**)

Defaults configurable in TOML. Also detect `interval_change` when median interval shifts beyond cadence tolerance.

Append billing row to `subscription_price_events` on every confirmed occurrence (not only on change) for ECharts and Grafana.

**Alert delivery:** in-app banner on `/subscriptions` + toast; defer global header bell to US-0005.

## Impact

- `price_change` submodule in subscriptions engine
- Config keys in `[subscriptions]` section
- Document promotional pricing as known false-alarm limitation

## Risks

- Promo end triggers increase alert when promo expires
- Foreign currency rounding on small deltas
- Annual billing: change detectable only after second year observation
